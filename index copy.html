About us

Acer child training center is an institution found in Bugesera district ,nyamata
which has many and different obligations/aims against the development of of the nation of Rwanda
It officially opened on 17th july 2021 after the very dangerous pandemic that was known to the rest of the world which was known as COVID-19.

Acer obligations.

  1. To reduce the number of uneducated people in the nation since the payments are affordable to
    all kinds of people.
  2. to promote numerous technical courses to the children of the nation typically since many technology machines have
    provided in the school community.
  3. To prepare the nation children future like preparing their careers with experienced personnel
    that will ease their search for jobs in future.
